About KishanMitra

Our Journey

This Project was started while participating in BUILDATHON organised by Club of Developers (CODE) , BIET Jhansi .
Idea of KishanMitra knocked when we decided to solve a real life problem using modern methods.
We decided to build a solution for Farmers and desired retailers to increase their income by just reaching out the correct audience. This project is still in its working stage and any query or suggestions will be positively welcomed.

How KishanMitra Helps You ?

Easy Business

Create Advertisements

This is the sections in which you will be able to find out all Advertisements that are available in your area . In this section what a Retailer do that he will create their ads as how much amount of crop he has present at how much price and post it, so if any Farmer will interested than he can easily connect with that Retailer.
Same case happens with farmer also that he will create an ad about that some amount of crop he is needed with their respective price and if any Retailer find out that ad is valuable for him then he will contact with that farmer. So this is the way of creating Advertisements.

Make New Connections

Now onwards while using KishanMitra every user will also be able to make new connections with each other. The farmers will be able to make good connection with Retailers and Retailers with farmers.
So the profit of making connections with each-other will be like Farmers can know easily while connecting with Retailers that which Retailer is giving that much crop at how much amount and same with the Retailer also that Retailers can know that how much amount a farmer will pay for their crops.
